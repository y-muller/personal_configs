### Leader key sequences

| `Leader` +        | Result
|-------------------|-------------------------
| `l`               | Select whole line
| `i`               | #include ❚
| `d`               | #define ❚

Markdown and HTML:
| `Leader` +        | Result
|-------------------|-------------------------
| `Esc`             | \`❚\`
| `Esc` + `Esc`     | \`\`\` <br> ❚ <br> \`\`\`
| `L Shift` + `l`   | \[❚\]\(\)
| `L Shift` + `i`   | \!\[❚\]()
| `L Shift` + `f`   | \[^❚\]
| `L Shift` + `b`   | \<b>❚\<\/b>

On selected text:
| `Leader` +        | Result
|-------------------|-------------------------
| `Spc` + `'`       | 'selection'❚
| `Spc` + `'` + `'` | "selection"❚
| `Spc` + `[`       | [selection]❚
| `Spc` + `[` + `[` | {selection}❚
| `Spc` + `]`       | [[selection]]❚
| `Spc` + `]` + `]` | {{selection}}❚
| `Spc` + `(`       | (selection)❚
| `Spc` + `)`       | ((selection))❚
| `Spc` + `8 *`     | \*selection\*❚

Text expansion:
| `Leader` +        | Result
|-------------------|-------------------------
| `x` + `c`         | _Company name_
| `c` + `x`         | _Company email_
| `x` + `d`         | _Company domain_
| `y` + `y`         | _Email_
| `d` + `d`         | _Domain 1_
| `m` + `m`         | _Domain 2_
